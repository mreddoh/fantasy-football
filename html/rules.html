The Scoring System:

Coaches can select players and teams, these two types of selections get points slightly differently.

  • Players get points by scoring goals. Each goal scored gets you a point.
    • For example, if you have Zach Tuohy in your team and he scores 6 goals, you get 6 points added to your score for the week.

  • A coach's lowest scoring player each week will not be counted towards their score (essentially acting like a sub).

  • Teams get points by limiting opposition goals.
    • If the team's opposition scores:
      • less than 6 goals then you score 8 points
      • less than 10 goals then you score 4 points
      • less than 15 goals then you score 2 points
      • more than 20 goals then you score -2 points


How the Draft is going to work:

Due to the new scoring system, the draft is altered slightly.

  • Draft picks will be in snake order (eg. 1,2,3,3,2,1) and a player can choose between picking a player or picking a team defence.
  • Each coach has to pick 1 team and 6 players (no more, no less).
  • No player or team can be chosen by two coaches.
  • All challenges to any draft picks need to be raised before the beginning of round one (check your teams!).


How Trading works:

Players can trade players in two different ways.

  1. Coach to Coach
    • You can make any number of player-for-player or team-for-team trades with another coach that agrees to the trade.
    • The right is reserved to deny any anti-competitive trades.

  2. Waiver Wire
    • Any player or team that is not in any coaches team can be traded for.
    • Priority of trades will be given in reverse-ladder order.
    • A maximum of two trades can be made each week.
    • A player or team that has been traded out will only be available on the waiver wire after the completion of the following round.


The Prizes:

2024 Buy-in is $30. Each winner of the 4 manager of the months (April, May, July, August) will win $30. While the winner will take the rest of the pot (assuming 8 entrants, $120).
